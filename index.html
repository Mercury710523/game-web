Count Masters: Stickman Games

Join the ultimate stickman battle where numbers decide your fate!

Game Overview

Count Masters: Stickman Games is an exciting multiplayer battle game where you control an army of stickman warriors. Collect stickmen, grow your numbers, and defeat enemy hordes in this addictive action-packed adventure.

The game combines strategy with fast-paced action, challenging you to make quick decisions as you navigate through various levels and obstacles.

How to Play

Controls

Simply drag your finger or mouse to move your stickman army. Collect number gates to multiply your stickmen, and avoid obstacles that can reduce your numbers.

Objective

Navigate through the levels, grow your army, and defeat all enemies. The bigger your army, the greater your chance of victory!

Features

  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Colorful graphics and smooth animations
  • Exciting boss battles
  • Free to play online

Tips and Strategies

Master the Multipliers: Always aim to go through the highest number gates possible to maximize your army size.

Avoid Obstacles: Watch out for spikes, traps, and negative multipliers that can drastically reduce your army.

Boss Strategy: When facing bosses, try to have as many stickmen as possible. Position yourself strategically to minimize losses.
